Stack Mobile Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 01, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Stack Mobile! This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our mobile application ("App"). Your privacy is critically important to us.

Our core principle is simple: Stack Mobile does not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on any external servers. All data processed by the App remains locally on your device.

Information We Do Not Collect

Stack Mobile is designed to function entirely offline with respect to your personal data. This means:

  • We do not collect any personal identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.
  • We do not collect device identifiers (like IMEI, MAC address, or advertising IDs).
  • We do not track your usage of the App for analytics purposes that would send data to our servers.
  • We do not have access to any content you create, manage, or store within the App.

Data Processed Locally

Any data that Stack Mobile handles, such as your settings, preferences, or any content related to the App's functionality, is stored exclusively on your mobile device. This data is under your control and is not accessible by us or any third party through the App.

If you choose to back up your device (e.g., via iCloud or Google Drive), data stored by Stack Mobile on your device might be included in that backup, subject to the terms and privacy policies of your backup service provider. We do not control this process.

Permissions

If Stack Mobile requests any permissions (e.g., access to storage, camera), it will be solely for the intended functionality of the app on your device. This data is processed locally and not transmitted off your device by Stack Mobile.

Third-Party Services

Stack Mobile does not integrate with any third-party services that would result in your personal data being sent from your device to an external server through our App.

Children's Privacy

Stack Mobile does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since we do not collect personal information in the first place, we do not solicit it from anyone, including children under the age of 13 (or the relevant age in your jurisdiction). If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us, although we reiterate that the App is designed not to transmit such data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
