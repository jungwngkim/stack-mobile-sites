Stack Mobile

Capture. Organize. Create.

Stack Mobile is designed to enhance your mobile photography experience, making it easier to capture stunning shots and turn your memories into compelling stories. We focus on intuitive tools that empower your creativity without overwhelming you.

1. Guided Photo Composition

Never struggle with framing your shots again! Stack Mobile provides simple, customizable shape overlays on your camera screen. These guides help you align elements, achieve better balance, and apply composition rules like the rule of thirds or leading lines, making your photos more visually appealing.

2. Collect Pictures as Albums

Keep your photographic projects and memories organized. Stack Mobile allows you to easily group related pictures into dedicated albums. Whether it's a vacation, a specific project, or a collection of your best shots, albums make it simple to find, view, and manage your photos.

3. Simple Timelapse Creation

Transform a series of photos from an album into a captivating timelapse video with just a few taps. Stack Mobile streamlines the timelapse creation process, allowing you to set the speed and generate a video directly within the app. It's an easy way to bring your photo sequences to life!
